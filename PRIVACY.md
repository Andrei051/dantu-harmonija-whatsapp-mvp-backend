# Privacy Policy - DH MVP WhatsApp Assistant

This Privacy Policy explains how the DH MVP WhatsApp Assistant processes data.

## Data We Process

- WhatsApp sender identifier (for example, phone/wa_id)
- Message content sent to the assistant
- Message metadata (for example, message ID, delivery status, timestamps)
- Technical logs used for reliability, security, and troubleshooting

## Why We Process Data

- To receive and process inbound WhatsApp messages
- To provide deterministic informational responses based on approved clinic website content
- To maintain service reliability, security, and auditability

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not provide diagnosis.
- We do not execute bookings or calendar actions.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for service operation and troubleshooting, and then deleted or anonymized according to operational needs.

## Sharing

Data is processed through infrastructure required to operate the service, including Meta WhatsApp Cloud API and hosting providers.
